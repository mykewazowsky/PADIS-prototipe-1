About

Project Purpose

The PADIS (Paddy Loss Information System) project aims to provide a simple and effective GIS-based dashboard for monitoring forest loss in Java, Indonesia. By visualizing geospatial data, it helps stakeholders understand deforestation trends, supports decision-making for conservation efforts, and promotes sustainable land management practices.

Academic Background

This project is developed as part of the Capstone Project in Geodesy and Geomatics Engineering at the Faculty of Earth Sciences and Technology (FITB), Institut Teknologi Bandung. It integrates principles of geodesy, remote sensing, GIS technologies, and data analysis to address real-world environmental challenges.
